Welcome to Infopeers

Infopeers is an end-to-end IT solutions and services company with vast experience across industry sectors with a mission to help our clients transform their business and is a uniquely customer centric software development, project management and consulting services company. Infopeers specializes in offering creative Software Outsourcing, Offshore Development and onsite staffing needs. We provide Outsourced Product Development services and customized software solutions to enterprises with special and unique needs. We combine fresh and creative ideas with specially structured processes and resources to produce best-quality software. Our on-site/offshore staff augmentative solutions are reliable, efficient, cost-effective and unique, ensuring competitive advantage for our clients. We strive not just to deliver cutting-edge software solutions, but also ensure that our services add value for our customers. We believe that understanding our clients and their strengths enables us to provide solutions, which would ideally suit their requirements, and add to their competitive advantage. We strive to create new opportunities for our clients through our solutions.Head quartered out of NY, InfoPeers has its overseas Global Development Center in India. We have extensive experience setting up Application Development Centers in all parts of the Globe allowing our clients a flexible approach to optimally meet their technical, operational and economical needs.
